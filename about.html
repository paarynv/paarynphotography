About Me

Who Am I?

My journey can be explained through two strands of life that eventually merged to form a passion for photography. On the one hand, I have loved wildlife since I was a child... wandering around the garden for insects from a young age, capturing spiders to keep in a vivarium, and eventually purchasing my very own corn snake aged 14 - Nala. Our family holidays became more and more countryside-focussed, and I quickly outgrew the family digital "point-and-shoot" camera. In 2022, I was gifted an entry-level DSRL: my trusty Canon EOS2000D!
